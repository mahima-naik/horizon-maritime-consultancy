Charting the Course for Future Mariners

Our Mission

To bridge the information gap and provide clear, actionable, and honest guidance for students aspiring to join the Merchant Navy.

Our Story

Navigating the transition from a standard high school curriculum to a specialized maritime academy can feel like sailing without a compass.Horizon Maritime was built on a deep understanding of the exact pressures facing 12th-grade science students.

We know that managing the heavy workload of a PCM combination while simultaneously trying to decode the IMU CET syllabus, physical standards, and sponsorship interviews is overwhelming. We created this consultancy to be the steady anchor you need during that chaotic process.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency: We give you the honest reality of life at sea—the hard work, the discipline, and the rewards. No false promises.
  • Precision: From medical checklists to application deadlines, in the maritime world, details matter. We ensure you never miss a step.
  • Dedication: Your goal is our goal. We stay with you from your first inquiry until you secure your cadetship.
